Medlert

Problem:

Medlert came to Fresh Tilled Soil with a current product that aims to be your personal 911 assistant on your smart phone. Through my time with Fresh Tilled Soil, we have done a UX deep dive to really understand the users and how they experience emergencies. Through the deep dive process, we identified the competitive landscape, design challenges, heuristic analysis, current onboarding process, personas,customers, users and pricing strategy. We looked at everything.

Solution:

What we found was that their current iPhone app and product was loaded with features that should not be there. Since Medlert is going to compete with 911 and other lifeline services, Medlert needs to be just as fast and reliable. The app needs to do one thing and do it better than 911 and thats launch an emergency faster and provide data on the user. The presentation on our research can be found here Medlert Design Strategy.

Geordie

Problem:

The morning commute with public transportation is a pain! You have to time everything making sure you dont miss your train and bus connections. Geordie, your personal commute Robot handles this for.

Solution:

The client was Fresh Tilled Soil and gave us very specific requirments on what the application should do. The most important requirment is telling the user when they should leave every day. I wanted to make sure the app did one thing and do it really well and thats tell you when you should leave. I wanted it to be a simple utility app that you can pull out every day, have a quick look and then its back in your pocket. Very simple and straight forward design.

Whoo!

Problem:

Web accessiblity is constantly over looked. This is a demonstration of how important it is to follow best practices for even the simplest and most common desgin patterns, a form.

Solution:

The code is written while keeping accessibiity in mind at all times. Screen readers, the ederly and anyone else with disablities should have the same pleasant expereince as the rest of the world. I am using a technique called progressive enhancement. Depending on your browser and your assets, the application will do different things to improve the over all user expereince. The credit card field autodetects the credit card number you enter in and matches it with your credit card company. I am using a keyup event through raw js and selecting the radio button. I am also using sprites for the images to cut down on those unessecarry http request. The produciont code was concatenated using Grunt.js.
